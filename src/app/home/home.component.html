Welcome To Our Dive Club!

We are Brighton’s friendly local dive club and one of the first BSAC branches, established in 1954 as Brighton BSAC club number 007!

We have some enthusiastic, experienced instructors at our club and welcome new members and divers from all diving agencies. From trainees who are new to the sport to experienced divers of all grades, divers who have learnt overseas and want to progress in the UK or guest divers from other clubs who wish to join us occasionally!

All are welcome… So lets dive in!

Dive With Us

Currently we are not offering try dives and training for new divers

Are you already a qualified diver from another agency but want to join a club environment instead?

Perhaps you qualified abroad on holiday and want to continue now you are home? We think its great to have our friends around us when we dive so we can share experiences together and would love for you to join us too! It doesn’t matter who you trained with, if you’ve never dived in the UK before or if you haven’t dived for a while – the doors are open to all !

Become a Guest Driver

OK, so you're qualified but want to 'test the waters' before joining. That's fine! You can join us for the day as a guest diver.

We ask that you meet one of the minimum qualifications:

  • BSAC Sports Diver
  • PADI Advanced Open Water
  • SAA Club Diver
  • IANTD Diver
  • And others!

We welcome guests who have qualified while in the UK or abroad, however we know that diving in the English Channel can be different to diving in the Red Sea for example, so please contact us if you have any questions or require any information as to the suitability of a dive.

During the summer we regularly dive an exciting range of local wreck and inshore drift dives. Keep an eye on our Club Calendar for dives we are planning.

Our dive fees are £20 per dive for guests and members. We welcome guests to dive with us up to 6 days per calendar year, or join the Club to continue the fun!

If you join us as a guest, the times you will be given are the boat departure times, so please allow yourself plenty of time (about an hour) to meet us, have the dive brief and get your kit ready. Typically a single dive trip will take approximately 3 to 4 hrs, but this can vary.

You will need to sign a UK Sport Diver Medical Form as a part of our procedures. We also require details of your dive history, so please bring your log book and certification.
