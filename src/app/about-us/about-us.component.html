About Us

Established in 1954 as Brighton BSAC 007, we are one of the first BSAC branches. Once based in Brighton, we are now based in Shoreham-By-Sea at the Sussex Yacht Club (SYC), giving easy access via the A27 and the train station to divers across Sussex, with members from Bognor Regis, Brighton, Falmer, Mid Sussex and Newhaven.

We have an 8 metre club boat (RIB) moored year round here at SYC which we take on trips to dive around the UK, space for storage of kit and an all-important bar and restaurant on site for diver debriefs! There is also free parking as well as changing and shower facilities.

Unlike many other clubs in the area, we also have our very own air compressor which gives our club members access to unlimited free air fills.

We get together every Wednesday evening from 8:00pm at the Sussex Yacht Club bar upstairs , come along and meet us for a drink! We’re a friendly bunch and love meeting new people! We can show you around the facilities and if you’re interested you can sign up for a Dive, Try Dive or Training Program with us.

Why People Choose Us

Brighton BSAC 007 is your one-stop high-quality , professional, customized and personal all-in-one training dive club. We take diving seriously and handle it intelligently realizing the deep joy in a warm social gathering to discover and enjoy a world of amazing dive sites in Shoreham-By-Sea at Sussex Yacht Club (SYC)

With a friendly and experienced dive team offering high-quality and memorable diving experiences, you will get fair prices and attractive packages for diving, courses and diving equipment to cater to your adventure with us.
